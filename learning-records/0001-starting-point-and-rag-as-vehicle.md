RAG is one of two L4 pillars (the other is DSA). He wants to start at the first RAG project even though the roadmap sequences it at Week 8 (after Python, Git, SQL, ML math, scikit-learn, PyTorch, LLM-API basics).

The grounding goal is defensible coding proficiency, not a deployed demo alone.

Implications for future sessions:
- Floor is L2, not L3+. Do not assume Python fluency; verify it before code-writing lessons.
- The classical/non-LLM flagship is required for the job hunt but is out of scope for this workspace (separate mission).
- No evidence of learning yet. This record sets the starting floor only; it is not proof of any acquired skill.
