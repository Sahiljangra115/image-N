MAYA'S BAKERY BINDER

Classic Vanilla Sponge
A light sponge made with flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and vanilla. Contains
dairy and eggs. No nuts are used in this recipe or its decoration. Safe for
customers with a nut allergy. Bakes at 180C for 25 minutes.

Almond Celebration Cake
A rich cake built on ground almonds and topped with flaked almonds. Contains
nuts and dairy. Not safe for anyone with a nut allergy. Often ordered for
weddings.

Lemon Drizzle Loaf
Made with flour, sugar, butter, eggs, and fresh lemon. Contains dairy and
eggs, no nuts. The drizzle is lemon juice and sugar only.

Supplier Notes
Flour comes from Greenfield Mills, delivered every Monday. Butter and eggs
come from Hill Farm. Almonds and other nuts are stored on a separate shelf to
reduce cross contamination, but the kitchen is not a nut-free environment.

Opening Hours
The shop opens Tuesday to Sunday, 8am to 5pm. Closed Mondays for deliveries
and prep.
